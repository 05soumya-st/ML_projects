# Units sold by brand
brand_sales = df.groupby('Car_Brand')['Units_Sold'].sum().sort_values(ascending=False)
brand_sales.plot(kind='bar', title='Units Sold by Brand', figsize=(8,5), color='skyblue')
plt.ylabel("Units Sold")
plt.xlabel("Brand")
plt.xticks(rotation=45)
plt.show()

📌 Insights:¶

  • Toyota had the highest total units sold.
  • Most cars are Petrol-based.
  • BMW cars are significantly more expensive.
  • Price does not directly correlate with units sold.
  • Manual transmissions are more common in this dataset.
